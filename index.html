Our Applications

Car Inspector

People shouldn't need to pay for expensive pre-purchase inspections to feel as though they're not buying a lemon. We help to guide people through a set of pre-purchase inspection steps that aims to identify common issues with cars.

We tailor each inspection instruction to the vehicle the person is wanting to purchase, and make the instructions as specific to that vehicle as possible. We then produce a report that attempts to help the buyer gauge how much they should be aiming to purchase the car for, using information from the inspection and car's history.
